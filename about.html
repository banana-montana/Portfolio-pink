I’m Lea - a graphic designer, illustrator and web designer from Cape Town in sunny South Africa. I use visual storytelling to transform complex information and ideas into powerful connections between people and your brand. I write code for the web and favour semantically structured markup, accessibility and well designed CSS . When I am not designing or coding mastermind projects, I am tending to my dying plants and my overly affectionate four cats.

I’d love to hear from you, even more so if it has to do with work. Drop me a mail, give me a tinkle or leave me your details and I’ll get back to you.
